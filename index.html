Our Story

Crafted for the
Nocturnal

After Hours started with a simple observation: Bhairahawa sleeps too early for its thinkers. We built a sanctuary for those who do their best work when the sun is down.

Your focus is our priority. We source 100% organic beans from the high-altitude plantations of Gulmi and Illam, roasting them in small batches to preserve the bold, earthy notes unique to Nepali soil.

This is not your typical high-street cafe. There is no loud music or bright lights here. We provide a minimalist atmosphere designed to fade into the background while you build your next big project.
